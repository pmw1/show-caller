# Media Directory

Place your standby videos here. They will persist across container restarts.

## File naming:
- `standby.mp4` - Default standby video for both slots
- `standby-slot1.mp4` - Specific standby video for slot 1
- `standby-slot2.mp4` - Specific standby video for slot 2

## Priority:
1. Slot-specific video (standby-slot1.mp4 or standby-slot2.mp4)
2. General standby video (standby.mp4)
3. Built-in generated video

## Video requirements:
- Format: MP4 with H.264 video and AAC audio
- Resolution: 1920x1080 recommended
- Frame rate: 30fps recommended
